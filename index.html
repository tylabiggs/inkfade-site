Meet the Founder

Hi, I’m Tyla — a creator, builder, and visionary who’s always questioned the idea of permanence. InkFade was born out of a moment of transition and identity transformation. After going through personal reinvention, I realized how many people crave the freedom to express without permanence holding them back. So, I began building a new category: semi-permanent tattoos that fade only when you choose. Together, we’re redefining what it means to wear your story on your skin.

Why It Matters

The tattoo industry has evolved for decades, yet permanence has always been its defining rule. What if self-expression didn’t have to last forever? Whether you outgrow your design, want to update your canvas, or simply crave flexibility, InkFade empowers you to write, rewrite, and reshape your story—on your timeline. Our proprietary ink formula bridges the gap between traditional permanence and fleeting trends, ensuring you never feel locked into a decision. This is the future of tattoos, where freedom and creativity coexist.

About the Founder

InkFade began as a vision born out of personal transformation. Created by Tyla Biggs, a driven entrepreneur who once felt boxed in by permanence — in life, choices, and ink. She envisioned a way for people to express themselves without lifelong commitment. That vision became InkFade: a rebellious alternative to traditional tattoos, giving people freedom to evolve.

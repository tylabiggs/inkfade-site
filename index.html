Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Tyla — a creator, builder, and visionary who’s always questioned the idea of permanence. InkFade was born out of a moment of transition and identity transformation. After going through personal reinvention, I realized how many people crave the freedom to express without permanence holding them back. So, I began building a new category: semi-permanent tattoos that fade only when you choose.

Where It All Began

InkFade was born from a single, burning question: What if tattoos could evolve with you?

Founded by Tyla Biggs—entrepreneur, creative force, and no stranger to reinvention—InkFade came to life in the middle of personal transformation. What started as an obsession with expression and commitment-free design turned into a category-shattering concept: semi-permanent tattoos that fade on demand, without the regret or the removal lasers.

Fueled by frustration with traditional tattoo permanence, and inspired by the idea that identity isn’t fixed, Tyla assembled a vision that now challenges the industry to rethink everything it thought it knew about ink.

Why It Matters

Every year, millions spend billions trying to erase tattoos they once loved. The regret industry is booming—while tattoo innovation has stayed stagnant.

Until now.

InkFade introduces a revolutionary ink technology designed to last 6–12+ months and fade when you're ready. No more compromises between self-expression and lifelong commitment. Our system blends real tattooing technique with fade-on-demand capability, merging the best of both permanence and freedom.

Development Timeline

Concept & Research

Validated market demand, studied dermatological limitations, and crafted an unserved niche.

Formula Development

Partnered with chemists to create a proprietary ink that mimics tattoo ink but is molecularly designed to fade cleanly over time—or on command.

Testing & Studio Trials

Studio collaborations to test application methods, fade timelines, and consumer perception.

Soft Launch

Limited rollout with curated partner studios to gather real-world data, testimonials, and refinement feedback.

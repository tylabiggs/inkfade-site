About

InkFade was born from one bold question:
What if tattoos could evolve with you?

Founded by Tyla Biggs — creative force, unapologetic visionary, and no stranger to reinvention — InkFade emerged during a season of transformation. What began as a fascination with commitment-free expression became a category-defining innovation: real tattoos that fade on demand. No regret, no lasers.

Driven by frustration with permanence and inspired by the truth that identity is never static, Tyla envisioned something the industry hadn’t dared to touch. InkFade doesn’t just improve ink — it rewrites the rules.

Real tattoos. Real fade. Full control.

Why It Matters

Every year, people spend billions trying to undo the ink they once believed in. Tattoo regret fuels a thriving removal industry. But tattoo innovation? It’s been stuck in the past.

InkFade changes that.

We’re developing a breakthrough ink system designed to last boldly for 6–12+ months — and fade when you’re ready. No more choosing between permanent expression and future regret.

This is real tattooing, powered by fade-on-demand freedom.
The same needle. The same bold look. But with a revolutionary twist: control.

Because identity evolves — and your ink should, too.
